The Real Heroes

by SOUMYADIP MUKHERJEE

In the present times where DC vs marvel rivalry brings in the perfect ammunition to the never dying flame of our craze for superheroes that are presented in a reel or a silver screen of a cinema theatre and we try to ambush and enjoy the glorification of those fictional characters, we often tend to forget that the real world in times like now with some of the most severe problems like climate change, nuclear world war, ecosystem imbalances, Deforestation, Biodiversity loss and many more…… we need heroes too and we ourselves have to become heroes of our society too.

Ya, the heroes that the society needs right now has to come up from ourselves, our society. As we believe , change could be brought only by changing the person by waking up the hero that lives within each of us and give them the superpowers. That superpower is nothing but a choice , a small choice in our daily life that can impact the world. While designing this website, we always argued that how can we make an impact to the world through this website and thus even this short fun quiz has a goal to hit. Quizzo is a alarm-clock that wants to wake up the superhero inside us, which superhero – that will depend on the choices you will make.
“With great power comes great responsibility”

Let's honor the heroes among us

At the end of the quiz, we have a very simple request which you are bounded to fulfill. Since you are a superhero now, bring a change , do something good for the society and we will feature you and your contribution in our “Hall of Fame” that will inspire more to come forward and be a part of this movement

With this movement that team Secreta hopes to bring in, there will be a positive change in the society, in the way people think, in the way people act that might be the linked domino of the big change in the world. Heroes you are, heroics you so.. Cheers to A Change.

-team Secreta
