The haunted past

Story 1

"Though I can't change what happened, I can choose how to react. And I don't want to spend the rest of my life being bitter and locked up. Past the mission, I smell the roses" -Tori Amos

Before I start-off with this confession, I have a point that I want everyone to be note:

If any girl or a child is alone then it's not opportunity but it is our responsibility, and we are responsible for their safety (repeating the same thing) because, some of us in this world are inhumane and evil enough to feed over the innocence to satisfy the darkness inside. Thus, if you find any such victim or know about that devil, please take steps and ensure that innocence does not die.

This story is an account of the tragic event deeply embedded in my past, from the period that dates back to when I was a kid/child in the literal expression (probably 7-8 years of age) and having been locked in form of nightmare and horrific memories for years now. I accept that I still haven’t been able to completely let-go-off it and ya, I guess probably never would in the future.

Beginning with my story, well not really a "story" .... but rather a "real horrific nightmare" experience with a monster that fed on my childhood innocence and left me with lots of scars to hide and heal for the rest of my life.

I need to introduce myself, well not the present me who is penning this down, but the kid me since this shall be a journey of the tragedy that kid faced.

This dates back to 1-st grader me, lively, notorious and very active in nuisances. I used to spend my day either playing or watching cartoons or destroying gardens or roaming streets and playing for the entirety of the day. My parents were very sweet ones, never pressurized me or beat me although scolded me a lot but it was not enough. They decided to admit me to a strict convent school such that I concentrate into my studies. Though, I didn't study. Most of my friends at school were either good at studies, or good at extracurricular or cultural activities (which were given a lot of importance in my school) or both. I felt a bit inferior among the others in my classes since I was average in my academics and zero in hobbies. Our class teacher used to give lot of appreciation and applause to the painters and artists and we had this kind of notice board attached at the three walls of the class to display the good paintings. I too wanted to be praised and applauded, and I wanted to learn painting. My papa was very welcoming towards my hobby and therefore got me admitted in an art school.

The teacher was a very famous artist and was quite active in conducting and judging various art contests for kids and elders too. He used to take drawing classes in his home and there were lots of children and elder ones who came to learn painting. He was a very amicable and super sweet person. He had short-medium height, a fat tummy and a bald head, with some amount of hair above ears. He used to wear golden frames and ride a green ladies bicycle. He used to give the small kids (like me) chocolates and also showed cartoons before class timings and very soon I became one of his favorites. The most unusual but remarkable thing about him that I still remember is his very creepy, disturbing smile, that was a constant all the time i have seen and known.

So i used to go to art classes to his home, which my papa used to accompany me every day, the class hours were in the morning (from 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM) and noon (from 2:30 pm to 4:30 pm) on weekends and maybe some extra classes on holidays in the mornings. The peculiar timing is obviously selected by me such that i don't miss my afternoon playing time. I don't remember now when things started very specifically, the fact that i have lost a lot of memories related to this due to trauma and maybe some locked deep inside my mind which I don’t and can't bring up. Yep, i was that kid-ish and childish and used to like drawing and paintings. I used to do fine sketching of cartoons (got selected for various sketching competitions and also awarded certificates and medals). I mostly used to love doing cartoon arts, and he used to praise me in front of my papa , or my friends in that art school (which was actually his home) and which child doesn't likes praises and applause at that age. For someone like me who didn't come first or second in exams, that praise was overwhelming. I used to love the drawing classes, because of two reasons. On some days, my papa used to give me the permission to ride cycle which i died for, and take me to ice cream shop since it was hot summer noon’s and hence the entire experience was great; Also not forgetting the afternoon games time which never got compromised.

So i used to reach there by 2pm and used to give extra time because sir asked my papa that it would be better for me if I practiced lil bit more than others. On most days it used to be just me alone in the class. There were a lot of kids who used to learn painting, usually about 30-40 in every class but they came in later. On days when i used to reach early, i used to watch cartoons on the tv and i used to sit on the bed. He used to stand mostly beside the bed and used to talk about games, schools, friends and other usual stuffs.

When I remained deeply lost in the cartoons, he used to place his hands on my thighs, the hands were very smooth and soft, probably because he did paintings. I used to wear half pants and t-shirts or thin shirts mostly. He used to place his hands on my thighs, and used to touch me at several places, occasionally rub his hands and used to grin looking at me.

Hugs and handshakes are awkward now. The darkness resides in me and I still have traumatizing nightmares and disturbing flashbacks.

The Facade

Story 2

I woke up with a jolt. These nightmares had been very frequent now. Sweat beads had covered my forehead and I was breathing hard. . I was relieved to know that the bad dream was over, just like the relationship. . But i couldn't stop thinking about all the things that had happened years before which still haunts me. I don’t know how many of us share this same real life story but I’m sure I are not alone and that many of us are facing very similar things each day.

It was in February of 2019 I met him, the most charming guy i had met till then.The first time we saw each other was in a shopping mall, I was wrapped in red, his favourite colour. We had a common friend who introduced us to each other and gave us the ocassion to meet again at her birthday. We were very comfortable with each other, he made me smile and laugh after so many years :) . He lived in another city so we exchanged our contact details and continued chatting with each other for months. We didn't meet each other a lot but he was already my best friend. He seemed different than other boys in many ways, gave me the personal space i needed, never ran out of things to say and always found something interesting to do. I was fascinated.

After a few months, he asked me for being his girlfriend. He was a great guy no doubt, but he had his breakup recently and I thought he should take some time first before taking any further decisions. Also I wasn't ready for a relationship either. So I politely refused him but he didn't give up. For a whole month he kept on trying to win my heart, became a great understanding friend and gave me the emotional support I wanted. After this i wasn't ready to lose him and said yes to his requests.

After spending a perfect month with him in relationship suddenly his ex returned one day, texted him that she would like to be back in relationship with him and demanded him to cut all connections with me. I was shocked when he agreed to this. But i knew the right thing to do now would be to leave them since they had been together for over a year and his and mine relation was just a month. Possibly he asked me to be in this relation to escape from his recent breakup and now that she returned, it was a complicated situation. I told him I would respect his ex’s decision and cut all communication with him. Our common friend was enraged upon hearing this and approached them to tell how wrong and unjust this was. Probably it made him feel guilty because after a few days he tried to contact me again, accepted his mistake and suggested that we could be friends again. He seemed very guilty and sad about this, I sympathized with his situation and agreed to this. After a week or so he came crying to me, saying how his ex’s return was a game she played and she was gone again leaving him alone. I tried to console him and be a good friend. He used this opportunity to manipulate me and pestered me with his requests to get back. I was furious initially but I don’t remember how did he managed to convince me once again that this time it was real and he had no feelings for his ex but only loved me. And so, I made the biggest mistake of my life and we were back!

The next few months of our relationship were great. He never seemed to be demanding or forcing. I gave him all my attention and time except my study hours. We used to play mobile games together and sometimes i played from his account too, to earn rewards for him. He loved being pampered and i loved watching him happy. We used to share all our secrets and emotional breakdowns due to various other reasons. Honestly, i was going through a lot of family problems and had grown to be lonely and concealed my feelings from the people around me. At this time, he became my pillar of support and lent me a pair of listening ears. I became extremely attached to him emotionally and we promised each other to be present for each other at any dark times of our life.

With time i realized he loved attention and was a pure extrovert. During the time when i got really busy he used to find new people to talk to and befriend them. He was never alone. In the meanwhile some of his lies got revealed. Although it was a small lie and did not matter to me, i wondered why did he need to lie (Now i know the reason of his lie, it was simply his nature). Also, he had became addicted to the attention and long hours of talking, so when I stayed busy during my exams he fought with me over this trivial issue and made it clear that whatever may be the reason if he is not given time then he will grow apart. I tried to follow this but sometimes it was just not possible. At those times he used to befriend new girls, and one girl among these gave me a weird vibe, I had this gut feeling that something was being hidden from me. I tried to reach that girl but it appeared that neither she nor he wanted me to talk to her much. Things became more and more suspicious

I had a strong feeling that things were very wrong and I needed to get to the bottom of this. I confronted him about this but it was of no use, he argued that I had no trust on him. Anyhow I managed to get the girls contact info and asked her in a friendly way to tell anything that I should know being his girlfriend but the girl simply denied everything (Although at a much later point she had accepted that they were close but he had manipulated her into believing that I was the villain and shouldn’t be told anything about this). He had sensed that I was alarmed and inquiring about this so he cut off his contact with the girl and convinced me that everything was normal and perfect.

By this time, I had started having trust issues and any slight change from the normal would alarm me. I had started acting like a spy. I tried to keep things as smooth as possible before I saw new activities in his ex’s social medias. I directly asked if he was still in contact with her and he tried to dodge the question which increased my suspicions. But I needed to be sure. There was no way his ex was going to talk to me since he had brain-washed her too. I asked my common friend to help and we managed to gather the proof that they were still talking. On confrontation, he accepted it and told me that his ex’s important exams were soon and he did not want her to go into depression now and that they were not exactly in a boyfriend-girlfriend relationship but rather friends. This made sense to me then and I asked what was his plan. He explained me that after her exams he would tell her every truth and reveal our relationship to her. For then the explanation worked for me but when her mid-semester exams were over I realized that he wasn’t talking about this exams but the final one which were after 2-3 months. This scared me. I couldn’t see the end of it. It seemed to me a bunch of lies and my trust in him was shaken. I started feeling insecure and worthless.

I started observing the little details now, spying to find out the truth. I wanted to be wrong so badly but nothing was helping, everything pointed that my suspicion was right. And finally, the day came when all of my doubts were cleared. His ex had uploaded a screenshot of their texts in social media which was visible to a few selected friends. Our common friend was luckily among them, she immediately sent the screenshots of those vulgar texts they had exchanged. I was shattered, I couldn’t believe that it was this far. The cheating was real and all those promises were a mere lie. In a single moment all the beautiful memories of the past 1 year seemed to disintegrate. I started seeing every truth hidden behind beautifully crafted excuses and expressions of love and care. Was it ever true? Was his feelings ever real? How long had he been dating both of us together? I wanted all the answers but before that I had a more important job to do. I needed to know if his ex knew any of this or if she had been similarly cheated because in the latter case she had the right to know the truth and hence I waited for her last exam to get over. In the meantime when I waited, I had stopped talking to him, blocked him from several places. He tried to contact me through many ways, called about 70 times before he sensed that now I was fully aware and wouldn’t listen any of his lies. Finally once his ex’s exams were over I talked to her and seeing her shock, already knew that we both had been cheated similarly. We both didn’t know about his relationship with the other one. She was enraged and furious and wanted revenge. But I was done thinking about him, I just wanted to stay alone, have some time to me, to mourn for the friend I thought I had.

But before it could end completely his ex and I got to know that he had started finding other people to fool. We teamed up and told those people the truth about him, the dirty game he played and tried to save them. After that I kept no contact with him but I’m in contact with his ex and sometimes check in on her if she is doing fine. The post breakup time wasn’t easy, after a very long time I was back to being alone, had no one to share my problems with, was emotionally broken and lost my trust in guys.

It took a long time but I’m over it and doing fine. I have one message for everyone, there are many people who are emotionally drained and broken and would do much better if someone stands beside them, listens to them patiently and give them a little support without expecting anything in return. Your one help could mean a lot to someone.

A Child of ignorance, fear and sadness

Story 3

Everyone probably has that time in their life when they felt vulnerable by some means. That was me in my 4th standard. I was 10 probably and a person struggling to make an image in front of the rest of my classmates and the teachers. I was not blessed with the best friend that I am at present. At that time, I had someone else to call a best friend who was bent on in probably showing me how inferior I was in front of the rest of the class and of course herself.

The Story goes like this, I was a child with no proper academic excellence and that I used to embarrassed myself every time in front of the whole class probably trying to make a mark.

I was feverish one day and had to remain in my house for nearly 4 days without going out anywhere as that little feverish tendency exceeded and I was in my bed. For this I was missing out my notes and also things that were probably important for my lessons. I might not be academically good but I was a tryer, I never wanted to beg or ask anyone for any sort of notes. I was pretty independent as a student and I also wanted to make sure that I take dictations in the class in the most efficient way possible. However, as I was not present in the class, I needed someone to help me out in taking down the notes. I had a landline in my house through which I connected with my so-called best friend who said that she would help me with anything. I believed in that and probably that was the biggest mistake that I have ever done in my life. I asked her to xerox whatever she has written in her copy as the notes given in the class was probably massive and as it is customary in the Indian schools, they give a lot of writing stuffs and a lot of notes and class work, so much that a student might bend over at that pressure.

What happened with me at that time was probably what I would not want for anyone else. That friend of mine said that there were no photocopy shops open at their region and that providing a copy to me for a day would not be that easy as she had to study and the weekend was near. Instead of that her mother copied each and every note in a different paper and gave it to me. That was a really sweet gesture and I appreciated it like anything. The problems escalated in the next month. The next month was the Friendship Day and I gave her a friendship band which she didn't like, she came and asked me why I gave that and that she needed a new one if there was a better friendship to be established. My mother was not having a proper income at that time and my mother was a single mother she had to work day and night for helping me survive and my studies to keep going on. She however afforded an even better friendship band. The next day the teacher was probably giving another note and as I said I was a person who took really fast dictations unlike my best friend who was a slow writer, she asked me for my copy and I asked her to let me write first and then shall I give her my copy for her to replicate it in her own sheet. Just because of the fact that I didn't give her my copy at that instant she said that I was not a good friend and that she would maintain distance from me even if I give her the logic necessary for me to copy so that she could write her own writings properly.

She however didn't understand what I meant. Then she started maintaining distance from me. I started crying on that matter as I was a very emotional child. This continued for more than a week or probably a month which I cannot recall. I used to sit with a friend of mine who was close to me but was separated just due to my so-called best friend’s vengeful act. She gradually started separating me from my other friends by telling them bad things about me and gradually cornered me up. She was one of the famous girls, as a result of which she and her words were believed by all. Not a single person was sympathetic towards me, be it my teacher or my friends. I was sad, probably depressed even if depression is a strong word. When I was at my home I was jolly however, every morning I used to wake up with tears in my eyes.

School was dreadful to me and I didn’t ever let anyone in my house understand what I was going through. My grandparents kept me entertained and sold it to anyone who came in and so did any near family member. Once I tried to hint my mother what was going on with me but she said it was just a primary school issue and that it must not be addressed in that manner and I being a responsible kid might have had that much capacity to deal with it. The situation had nothing to do with me as it was not just a little friendly tussle or such.

One day another teacher entered our class, she was a new teacher who noticed how bad I was doing in the class and how I was not like any other child participating in anything that was good. Literally I was on my own for a month and no one was there to protect me. This new teacher called my mother one day and explained her the whole thing that was going on with me which she probably thought was affecting my studies and me as a whole. She came in my life as a person who was probably there to pull me out from The Darkest corners of my life at that time. The time when I tormented myself, the time when I thought I should not even exist, the time when I thought I should not be even born. It must however not seem that I was struggling over a friendship but I was struggling over bullying, ignorance and the harshness of the situation that my mom at the time was in.

Standing today I feel how could have I been here if I would not have been fighting for my own existence back then. Then must I also say how the situation affected me. Her little mischief made me prey on her like a person who used to demotivate her while doing any possible thing.

That the anger that I had in myself should not be casted on anyone else.
